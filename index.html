HOW WE DO IT

Every quarter,we onboard a new cohort of entrepreneurs into our incubation program and
mentor them throughout their journey.We aim to provide financing for software devlopment
through equity financing,loans and grants.We follow the following steps to select the best
teams and build the best-in-class products.

  • application

    Teams looking to join our next cohort should apply here. We process dozens of Applications weekly and will get back to you as soon as possible once we review your pitch deck.

  • interview

    Once we have reviewed your application, you should expect a short call with one of our program managers. The goal is to screen the founding team and business idea further to ensure we understand the vision.

  • market research

    Based on a collected information,our research team analyzes the market to introduce your ideas in the big picture,including competitor analysis,use-cases preparation, expense calculation,infrastructure cost,and many more.

  • get ready

    Once we have agreed on a product, roadmap, and vision, our team will determine the scope of work involved in shipping the product. then, we will provide a team of devlopers, designers, and product/program managers to devlop the scoped projects.
